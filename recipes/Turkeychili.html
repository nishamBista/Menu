Turkeychili

There is a lot to love about a classic lasagne, with oozing layers of delicious beef mince covered in tomato sauce and topped with cheese, it's clear why this hearty meal is universally loved.

  • 500 grams fresh lasagne pasta sheets
  • 2 cups (160g) finely grated parmesan
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 medium brown onion (150g), chopped finely
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/400°F. Grease a deep 25cm x 35cm (10-inch x 14-inch) baking dish.
  2. Make bechamel sauce: Melt butter in a medium saucepan, add flour; stir until mixture forms a smooth paste.
  3. Bake lasagne for 40 minutes or until top is browned lightly. Stand for 15 minutes before cutting.
